What happened to GiftCardMall?

In 2025, Blackhawk Network — the company that operates GiftCardMall — officially rebranded GiftCardMall.com and merged it into Giftcards.com. This was a deliberate business decision to unify all of Blackhawk Network's consumer-facing gift card platforms under a single brand.

GiftCardMall had been operating since the early 2000s as one of the leading US gift card retailers and managers. The rebrand reflects the company's strategy to consolidate its digital presence and offer a more unified experience for consumers.

This is not a shutdown — it's a rebrand. All cards, services, and customer support continue to operate normally, just under the Giftcards.com name.

✅ Your money is safe

The rebrand has no effect on card balances. Every dollar loaded onto your GiftCardMall MyGift card is still there and spendable.

What this means for your existing cards

If you have a GiftCardMall MyGift card — whether it's a Visa or Mastercard — your card continues to work exactly as before. The physical card hasn't changed. The balance hasn't changed. The card network hasn't changed.

The only thing that changed is where you go online to manage your card. Instead of GiftCardMall.com, you now use Giftcards.com.

Do I need to re-register my card?

If you had an account on GiftCardMall.com, you may need to create a new account on Giftcards.com and re-register your cards. Blackhawk Network has advised customers to check their account status at Giftcards.com. The registration process takes about 2 minutes.

What about mygift.giftcardmall.com?

The old URL mygift.giftcardmall.com still technically exists (it's used by Blackhawk Network for certain card operations) but the primary consumer-facing portal for managing cards is now giftcards.com.

The back of older cards may still print the old URL. If you navigate there, you may be redirected to the new site, or you may reach a legacy Blackhawk portal. For simplicity and security, always go directly to giftcards.com.

⚠️ Beware of typosquatting sites

The rebrand has created confusion, which scammers exploit. Fake sites mimicking GiftCardMall or Giftcards.com have appeared in search results. Always type the URL directly into your browser — never click an ad for card activation.

What you should do now

  1. Bookmark giftcards.com as your new go-to for all GiftCardMall MyGift card management.
  2. Check your balance at giftcards.com to confirm everything transferred correctly.
  3. Re-register your card if you had a GiftCardMall account — this protects you if the card is lost or stolen.
  4. Update any saved logins or bookmarks pointing to GiftCardMall.com.

Watch out for fake sites

The rebrand has created significant confusion, and search engines unfortunately rank some fake "informational" or phishing sites alongside legitimate ones. Here's how to stay safe:

  • Always type URLs directly into your browser bar
  • Look for the HTTPS padlock (🔒) before entering any card details
  • The official site is giftcards.com — not any variation or lookalike
  • If you think you entered details on a fake site, call 1-833-800-0550 immediately to freeze your card
