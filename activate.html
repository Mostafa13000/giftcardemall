How to Activate Your
GiftCardMall MyGift Card

Step-by-step guide — takes about 2 minutes. Updated for 2026.

🔔

Important: GiftCardMall is now Giftcards.com

As of 2025, GiftCardMall has officially merged with Giftcards.com. All activation, balance checks, and account management now happen at giftcards.com. Your existing cards are fully supported — nothing expires or changes for you.

Step-by-step activation guide

  1. Gather your card information

    Before you start, have your card ready. You'll need: the 16-digit card number on the front, the expiration date (MM/YY), and the CVV security code (3 digits on the back).

    Tip: If your card arrived in a packaging envelope, keep the envelope — it sometimes has important details or a PIN you might need later.

  2. Go to the official activation website

    Open your browser and navigate to giftcards.com — this is the official site that replaced GiftCardMall. Do NOT use third-party sites. The back of your card may still say "mygift.giftcardmall.com" — that URL now redirects to giftcards.com.

    Security tip: Always double-check you're on the correct URL before entering card details. Look for the padlock (🔒) in your browser address bar.

  3. Find the activation or balance section

    On Giftcards.com, look for the "Manage Card" or "Activate" option. You may be prompted to create a free account or continue as a guest — either works for activation.

  4. Enter your card details

    Type in your 16-digit card number, the expiration date, and the CVV code. Double-check each field before submitting — a typo is the most common reason activation fails.

    Online purchases: If you plan to use the card online, you'll also need to register a billing ZIP code. Use the ZIP code of your home address or the address associated with your account.

  5. Register your card (strongly recommended)

    Creating a free account and registering your card protects you if the card is ever lost or stolen. Without registration, a lost card cannot be replaced. Registration takes about 60 seconds.

⚠️ Watch out for fake sites

Search results sometimes surface fake "activation" sites that steal card details. Always type giftcards.com directly into your browser — never click an ad to activate your card. The legitimate site always has HTTPS (🔒) in the address bar.

What you'll need

✅ Required info

  • 16-digit card number (front of card)
  • Expiration date (MM/YY)
  • CVV / security code (back of card)
  • Your ZIP code (for online use)

🛡️ Optional but recommended

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Full name and address
  • Phone number
  • Password for your account
