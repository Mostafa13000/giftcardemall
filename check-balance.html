Check Your GiftCardMall MyGift Balance

Three easy ways to see how much is left on your card — online, by phone, or at a store.

Fastest method. Visit giftcards.com and check your balance instantly.

Method 1 — Check balance online

🌐 Online via Giftcards.com

  1. Open your browser and go to giftcards.com (the official site that replaced GiftCardMall)
  2. Click on "Manage Card" or "Check Balance" in the navigation
  3. Enter your 16-digit card number, expiration date, and CVV
  4. Your current balance and recent transaction history will be displayed
💡 If you've registered your card, log into your account to see a full transaction history and statement — useful for tracking exactly where you spent your card balance.

Method 2 — Check balance by phone

📞 Call customer service

  1. Call 1-833-800-0550 — available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  2. Select the balance inquiry option from the automated menu
  3. Enter your card number using your phone keypad
  4. Your balance will be read out automatically — no waiting for an agent needed
💡 The phone number on the back of your card also works. Both lines connect to the same Blackhawk Network customer service system.

Method 3 — Check at a store

🏪 In-store balance inquiry

  1. Visit any store that accepts Visa or Mastercard (depending on your card type)
  2. Hand your card to the cashier and ask them to run a balance inquiry
  3. Many point-of-sale systems can display the remaining balance before completing a transaction
  4. Alternatively, make a small purchase — the receipt will usually show your remaining balance
💡 This method doesn't require internet access and is useful if you're already at a store and want to know if you have enough to cover your purchase.

Balance check tips

  • Check before shopping online — some merchants put temporary authorization holds that can confuse your available balance.
  • Gas stations and hotels often hold $50–$100 more than your actual purchase for authorization — your real balance comes back within a few days.
  • Split payments — if your card doesn't have enough to cover a full purchase, ask the merchant if you can pay part with the gift card and the rest with another card.
  • Register your card — once registered at giftcards.com, you can see a full transaction history online at any time.

Having trouble checking your balance? See our troubleshooting guide or call 1-833-800-0550.
